# ThirtyFoundry roadmap

| Day | Product | Audience | One-line pitch |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | ScopeSignal | Freelance designers and developers | Price a scope change and explain its schedule impact before saying yes. |
| 2 | Turnover Tally | Independent property managers | Coordinate every turnover task, vendor, and cost in one handoff view. |
| 3 | Route Margin | Mobile detailers and lawn-care operators | Quote profitable stops using drive time and route density. |
| 4 | Session Brief | Independent coaches | Collect a focused client agenda before every coaching call. |
| 5 | Deposit Decider | Wedding photographers | Match deposits and cancellation terms to real booking risk. |
| 6 | Cottage Label | Cottage food producers | Build consistent ingredient and allergen labels from reusable recipes. |
| 7 | CleanBid | Residential cleaning companies | Produce room-aware cleaning estimates without missing labor multipliers. |
| 8 | Booth Break-Even | Craft fair vendors | Know the sales target that makes an event worth attending. |
| 9 | Petty Cash Paws | Pet sitters and dog walkers | Reconcile mileage, keys, and pass-through client costs. |
| 10 | Inspection Window | Home inspectors | Schedule realistic inspection windows from travel and property complexity. |
| 11 | Kiln Queue | Community pottery studios | Track shared firings, fees, shelf space, and pickup readiness. |
| 12 | Tutor Pack | Private tutors | Keep lesson packages, cancellations, and parent updates aligned. |
| 13 | Permit Pulse | Small remodeling contractors | Give clients a clear, repeatable view of permit dependencies. |
| 14 | StemCount | Independent event florists | Turn floral recipes into purchase quantities with a spoilage buffer. |
| 15 | Chair Rent ROI | Salon chair renters | See true service margins after chair rent and consumables. |
| 16 | Covenant Draft | Self-managed HOAs | Create consistent violation notices with a defensible action history. |
| 17 | PrepWheel | Single-truck food vendors | Forecast event prep from covers, menu mix, and conditions. |
| 18 | UsageQuote | Freelance voice actors | Quote usage rights and renewal terms with confidence. |
| 19 | Pool Dose Log | Independent pool technicians | Record readings, chemical doses, and customer proof per visit. |
| 20 | SetClock | Working cover bands | Build venue-ready sets that actually fit the clock. |
| 21 | Notary Trip | Mobile notaries | Quote travel and wait time while respecting fee caps. |
| 22 | ShareSwap | Small CSA farms | Handle vacation holds and box swaps without inbox churn. |
| 23 | Intake Delta | Solo massage therapists | Ask returning clients only what changed since their last intake. |
| 24 | PartsMargin | Independent appliance repair shops | Protect margin across parts, shipping, warranty risk, and labor. |
| 25 | Grant Match Grid | Tiny nonprofits | Eliminate ineligible grants before investing in an application. |
| 26 | ClassCap | Microgym owners | Reveal class profitability after coaches, no-shows, and memberships. |
| 27 | Wholesale Ready | Handmade product businesses | Validate wholesale margin, lead time, and minimum-order terms. |
| 28 | PlowProof | Independent snow-removal operators | Create timestamped service and salt-use records for every stop. |
| 29 | Venue Overtime | Small event venue managers | Settle overtime, add-ons, deposits, and damages without disputes. |
| 30 | CareVisit | Gravesite care services | Give remote families scheduled care records and before/after proof. |

Scoring in `roadmap.json` uses ten 1–5 dimensions: pain, frequency, willingness to pay, reachability, buildability, demoability, retention, differentiation, automation fit, and low support burden.
